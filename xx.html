BS Cybersecurity Program

COMSATS University Islamabad offers a comprehensive 4-year BS Cybersecurity program designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to protect digital infrastructures. The curriculum covers topics such as network security, ethical hacking, cryptography, and cyber law.

The BS Cybersecurity program consists of 8 semesters (4 years) in total. Each semester includes both theoretical courses and practical labs.

Subjects Studied in BS Cybersecurity

  • Semester 1: Introduction to IT, Programming Fundamentals, Calculus, English Composition, Pakistan Studies
  • Semester 2: Object-Oriented Programming, Discrete Structures, Communication Skills, Linear Algebra, Islamic Studies
  • Semester 3: Data Structures and Algorithms, Computer Networks, Database Systems, Operating Systems, Technical Writing
  • Semester 4: Web Technologies, Network Security, Probability and Statistics, Digital Logic Design, Cyber Laws and Ethics
  • Semester 5: Information Security, Penetration Testing, Mobile & Wireless Security, Human-Computer Interaction, Research Methods
  • Semester 6: Applied Cryptography, Malware Analysis, Secure Software Engineering, Cloud Security, Project-I
  • Semester 7: Digital Forensics, Security Audits, Blockchain Security, Elective I, Project-II
  • Semester 8: Artificial Intelligence in Security, Cyber Threat Intelligence, Elective II, Final Year Project (FYP)

Graduates of this program are prepared for careers in cybersecurity analysis, information security management, and digital forensics.
